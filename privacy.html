Plain-Language Summary
When you join the Popcorn Universe waitlist, you share your name, email address, and city with us. We use this information only to communicate with you about the app, upcoming features, and entertainment content that may interest you. We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for their own marketing purposes. You may unsubscribe or request deletion of your data at any time.

01About This Policy & Who We Are

This Privacy Policy describes how Popcorn Universe Inc. ("Popcorn Universe", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information provided through our waitlist registration at popcornuniverse.ca and any associated communications.

Popcorn Universe is a Canadian technology startup developing an all-in-one entertainment booking application for movies, live theatre, stand-up comedy, concerts, and sporting events across Canada. We are headquartered in Waterloo, Ontario, Canada.

This policy has been prepared in accordance with Canada's federal privacy law — the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), R.S.C. 2000, c. 5, and Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL), S.C. 2010, c. 23. By submitting your information through our waitlist form, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

02What Information We Collect

When you sign up for the Popcorn Universe waitlist, we collect only the following personal information. We practice data minimization — we collect no more than what is necessary for the stated purposes.

Data Type What We Collect Why It Is Needed
Full Name First and last name as entered in the form To personalize communications (e.g., "Hi Nishant") and maintain accurate waitlist records
Email Address Personal or professional email address Primary channel for sending waitlist confirmations, product updates, and email campaigns you have consented to receive
City City or region within Canada (e.g., "Toronto", "Waterloo") To understand geographic demand, prioritize launch markets, and ensure communications are relevant to your region
Timestamp Date and time of waitlist submission To maintain a verifiable consent record as required under CASL and PIPEDA
Consent Record Source of signup, IP address hash (anonymized) To document express consent in compliance with CASL. The IP address is anonymized — we do not store your full IP address.
What We Do Not Collect
We do not collect payment information, government identification, health data, date of birth, phone numbers, social media identifiers, precise location data, device identifiers, cookies, or any sensitive personal information as defined under PIPEDA at the waitlist stage.

03Why We Collect Your Information

Under PIPEDA Principle 2 (Identifying Purposes), we are required to identify the purpose for collecting personal information at or before the time of collection. The purposes for collecting your name, email address, and city are:

  • Waitlist Management: To maintain an accurate, ordered record of individuals who have expressed interest in Popcorn Universe ahead of our public launch.
  • Email Communications: To send you relevant updates, announcements, and campaigns as described in Section 4 below, for which you have provided express consent.
  • Market Analysis: To conduct internal, anonymized analysis of geographic demand across Canadian cities, which informs our launch sequencing and feature prioritization. This analysis uses aggregated, anonymized data and does not identify individual users.
  • Business Development: To understand and report on early audience interest to potential investors, partners, and internal stakeholders. Any reporting uses aggregate statistics only — no individual personal data is disclosed.
  • Product Development: To inform which cities, entertainment categories, and features to prioritize in the Popcorn Universe app. Insights are derived from anonymized, aggregated data (e.g., "42% of waitlist signups are from Toronto").

We will not use your personal information for any purpose other than those stated above without first obtaining your explicit consent for the new purpose.

Your email address is the primary channel through which we communicate with you. By submitting the waitlist form, you expressly consent to receiving the following categories of Commercial Electronic Messages (CEMs) as defined under CASL:

  • Waitlist Confirmation: A thank-you email confirming your successful registration on the Popcorn Universe waitlist, including your position and what to expect next.

  • App Launch Announcement: Notification when the Popcorn Universe mobile app becomes available for download, including early access instructions for waitlist members.

  • Exclusive Pre-Launch Content: Sneak peeks, behind-the-scenes updates, and exclusive previews of the Popcorn Universe app experience.

Future Opt-In Campaigns

  • Additional Email Campaigns: Any future email campaigns beyond the categories listed above (such as promotional offers, loyalty programs, or partner announcements) will only be sent if you separately and explicitly opt in at the time those campaigns are introduced. Your consent to the waitlist does not constitute consent to all future marketing communications.
Important — Your Email Is Yours
Your email address will never be sold, rented, traded, or transferred to any third party for their independent marketing, advertising, or outreach purposes. Full details are in Section 6 (Data Sharing & Third-Party Disclosure).

05Consent — How We Obtain & Record It

Under both PIPEDA Principle 3 (Consent) and CASL Section 10, consent must be express, informed, and freely given. Popcorn Universe uses express opt-in consent — the highest standard recognized under Canadian law.

How We Obtain Consent

When you submit the waitlist registration form, you are presented with a clear and accurate description of what you are consenting to, including the categories of emails listed in Section 4. Submission of the form constitutes your express consent to receive those communications. Checkboxes are never pre-ticked. No consent is assumed by default.

What We Record

As required by CASL, we maintain a verifiable consent record for each signup that includes:

  • Date and time of form submission
  • The specific consent language presented at the time of signup
  • Source URL of the form (our waitlist page)
  • An anonymized identifier confirming the submission originated from a human interaction

Renewing Consent

If we identify a new purpose for using your personal information that was not disclosed at the time of collection, we will contact you to obtain fresh, informed consent for that new purpose before proceeding. We will not use your data in ways that were not clearly described at signup.

06Data Sharing & Third-Party Disclosure

Under PIPEDA Principle 5 (Limiting Use, Disclosure, and Retention), we will not use or disclose your personal information for purposes other than those for which it was collected, except with your consent or as required by law.

We Do Not Sell or Share Your Data

Popcorn Universe will never sell, rent, trade, or license your personal information — including your email address, name, or city — to any third party for their own marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes. This is a firm commitment and a core principle of how we operate.

Service Providers (Data Processors)

We may use carefully selected third-party service providers to assist with specific technical functions necessary to operate our waitlist and email communications. These providers act strictly as data processors on our behalf, meaning they may only process your data according to our written instructions and for no other purpose. They include:

  • Email Delivery Services: To send transactional and marketing emails (e.g., a platform like Mailchimp, Klaviyo, or similar). These providers receive your email address and name for the sole purpose of delivering emails you have consented to receive.
  • Data Storage & Hosting: Secure cloud infrastructure providers (such as AWS or Google Cloud) to store waitlist data securely.
  • Analytics Tools: Anonymized, aggregated analytics to understand engagement with our emails (e.g., open rates, click rates at an aggregate level). No individual user data is profiled.

All service providers are contractually bound to handle your data in accordance with PIPEDA, maintain appropriate security safeguards, and immediately notify us of any data breach.

Legal Disclosure

We may disclose your personal information without your consent only where required by law — for example, if compelled by a valid court order, warrant, or other legally binding government authority. In any such case, we will disclose only the minimum information necessary and will notify you as permitted by law.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of Popcorn Universe's assets, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. You will be notified by email prior to such a transfer and given the opportunity to withdraw consent and request deletion of your data.

07Data Retention & Deletion

Under PIPEDA Principle 5, personal information shall be retained only as long as necessary for the fulfilment of the purposes for which it was collected.

Data Retention Period Basis
Waitlist records (name, email, city) Until you unsubscribe or request deletion, or until the waitlist is closed (whichever is earlier) Necessary to fulfill the purpose of the waitlist and deliver consented communications
Consent records (timestamp, source) 3 years from the date of consent, or as required by CASL CASL compliance — proof of consent must be maintainable for the duration of the relationship and for a reasonable period after
Unsubscribe records Retained indefinitely as a suppression list To ensure we never re-contact individuals who have withdrawn consent, as required by CASL
Anonymized analytics data Indefinitely (no personal data retained) Fully anonymized aggregate data does not constitute personal information under PIPEDA and may be retained for business intelligence purposes

When your data is no longer required, it will be securely deleted or rendered permanently anonymous using industry-standard methods. We do not archive personal data beyond its stated retention period.

08Security Safeguards

Under PIPEDA Principle 7 (Safeguards), personal information must be protected by security safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the information. Popcorn Universe implements the following technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher (HTTPS). We do not accept unencrypted connections.
  • Encryption at Rest: Waitlist data stored in our systems is encrypted at rest using AES-256 or equivalent standards.
  • Access Controls: Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only, on a strict need-to-know basis. All access is logged and reviewed.
  • Data Minimization: We collect and retain only the minimum personal data necessary. We do not store full IP addresses, device fingerprints, or behavioral tracking data at the waitlist stage.
  • Third-Party Security Obligations: All service providers are contractually required to maintain security standards consistent with or exceeding our own.
  • Breach Response: In the event of a security breach involving your personal information that poses a real risk of significant harm, we will notify you and the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada (OPC) promptly, in accordance with PIPEDA's mandatory breach reporting requirements.
  • Regular Review: Our security practices are reviewed regularly as our systems and threat landscape evolve.
Data Breach Notification
Under PIPEDA's mandatory breach reporting rules (in force since November 2018), Popcorn Universe is legally required to notify affected individuals and the OPC of any breach that poses a "real risk of significant harm." We will act promptly and transparently in any such event.

10Unsubscribe & Withdrawing Consent

Under CASL Section 11, every commercial electronic message we send must include a clearly visible, functional unsubscribe mechanism. Popcorn Universe complies with this requirement in the following ways:

  • Unsubscribe Link in Every Email: Every marketing and promotional email we send includes a clear "Unsubscribe" link in the footer. Clicking this link will immediately record your opt-out request.
  • 10-Business-Day Processing: As required by CASL, we will give effect to all unsubscribe requests within 10 business days of receipt. You will not receive further commercial electronic messages after this period.
  • Email-Based Requests: You may also unsubscribe by sending a request to privacy@popcornuniverse.ca with "Unsubscribe" in the subject line. Please include the email address you wish to unsubscribe.
  • Suppression List: Upon unsubscribing, your email address is added to our suppression list. This means it is retained solely to ensure we never contact you again — it is not used for any other purpose.
  • Partial Opt-Out: Once we offer multiple types of email campaigns, you will have the option to unsubscribe from specific campaign types (e.g., promotional emails) while continuing to receive others (e.g., critical account notifications).
Note on Transactional Emails
Certain emails — such as a direct response to a question you've sent us or a legally required communication — may be sent regardless of marketing opt-out status, as they are not classified as commercial electronic messages under CASL.

11Children's Privacy

The Popcorn Universe waitlist and associated communications are intended for individuals who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted personal information to our waitlist without your consent, please contact us immediately at privacy@popcornuniverse.ca. We will take prompt steps to verify the situation and, if confirmed, delete the relevant information from our records.

When the Popcorn Universe app launches, age-gating and enhanced parental consent mechanisms will be implemented in accordance with applicable Canadian law for any features or content accessible to minors.

12Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our practices evolve, as the Popcorn Universe service expands, or as required by changes in Canadian privacy law.

Under PIPEDA Principle 8 (Openness), we are obligated to make information about our privacy policies and practices readily available. When we make material changes to this policy, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date
  • Send an email notification to all waitlist subscribers describing the nature of the change
  • Where a change involves a new use of your personal information, obtain fresh consent before that new use begins

Your continued use of our services or continued membership on the waitlist after we post an updated policy constitutes your acknowledgment of the changes. Non-material changes (such as formatting updates or clarifications that do not affect how your data is used) will be posted without individual notification.

13Regulatory Framework & Compliance References

This Privacy Policy has been prepared with reference to the following Canadian laws, regulations, and official guidance:

  • PIPEDA: Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act, R.S.C. 2000, c. 5 — Canada's primary federal private-sector privacy law. Governs the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information in the course of commercial activities.
  • CASL: Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation, S.C. 2010, c. 23 — Governs the sending of commercial electronic messages, requiring express or implied consent, sender identification, and unsubscribe mechanisms.
  • OPC Guidelines: Office of the Privacy Commissioner — Guidelines for Obtaining Meaningful Consent (May 2018).
  • CRTC Guidance: CRTC Guidance on CASL Implied Consent — Canadian Radio-Television and Telecommunications Commission.
  • ISED CASL Guidance: Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada — Getting Consent to Send Email.
  • PIPEDA Mandatory Breach Reporting: Breach of Security Safeguards Regulations, SOR/2018-64 — Governs our obligation to report privacy breaches to the OPC and notify affected individuals.

Popcorn Universe is accountable under PIPEDA Principle 1 (Accountability) for all personal information under its control. Our Privacy Officer is responsible for ensuring compliance with these obligations and for handling all privacy-related inquiries and complaints.
